Knowledge Hub: All About Battery Repurposing

❓ How long does the repurposing process take?

It usually takes 5-7 days depending on the battery condition and inspection results.

⚡ Can all battery types be repurposed?

We primarily handle Lithium-ion, Lead-acid, and Nickel-Cadmium batteries. Other battery types may be evaluated case by case.

📦 What happens if a battery is unfit for repurposing?

Unfit batteries are safely disposed of following strict environmental regulations to prevent contamination.

🔋 What are the benefits of repurposing batteries?

Battery repurposing reduces e-waste, minimizes environmental impact, and provides affordable energy solutions.

📊 How can I track my repurposing progress?

You can track your collection and repurposing progress via the Dashboard under the ‘Track Progress’ section.
